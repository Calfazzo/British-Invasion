The "Acoustic" British Invasion

More than a cover artist, Sean Farrell as THE "ACOUSTIC" BRITISH INVASION is a musical experience. He has been playing music for more than 30 years. Starting his musical career in the 80`s in the Rock-Metal-Punk band, based in Leicestershire England. "HEY EDNA" A move to Frankfurt Germany saw Sean join the Covers band "THE CHAPTER". And now living in GA USA, trading vocals and styles as he moves from one genre to another, recreating hits of today and classics from decades past.
